NetSuite ERP Discovery

Standardized requirement gathering forms for NetSuite implementation projects. Select an area below to begin your discovery session.

How to Use

  1. Select a discovery area below to open the questionnaire
  2. Fill in the Session Info at the top (client name, your name, date, attendees)
  3. Complete each section during the discovery session — track progress with the bar at the top
  4. Save your work — click "Save to Google Drive" to upload the completed form directly to the project folder, or "Export to Excel" to download locally
